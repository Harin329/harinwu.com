Take5 - Decisions Assistance

Dec 2018 - Jan 2019



PRODUCT:

Decision Maker App

TECHNOLOGY:

Swift

COMPANY:

Personal Project

DESCRIPTION:

My first attempt at publishing and creating a full product. However, I didn't want to make a basic game like every other Computer Science student. I wanted to make something I could genuinely use: Take5 - Decisions Assistance. Being a very indecisive individual, this basic yet effective application makes decisions based on user input and user subconscious through simple mathematical and statistical calculations with preset weights and logical statements. A randomizer at its simplest, I can make rational decisions easily with the help of questions, past experiences and a pros/cons list. It is so effective and simple that I use it to make many of my decisions clearly to this day!
