Product:

Pokemon TCG Purchase Helper

TECHNOLOGY:

Vite, SWC, ReactJS

COMPANY:

Personal Project

With so many Pokemon Trading Card Game expansion packs available at any given store, this tool was created to help make the best purchasing decision. The user can enter all the available expansions at the store, and the app will calculate the live average card price to help decide which pack will have the highest hit.

Expansions do not always have the same value, as this app proved. In 2023, Pokemon company workers stole cards off the production line to sell. Pokemon confiscated and destroyed these cards, ultimately causing a set of cards in Fusion Strike boosters to gain sudden value. This was able to be detected with Pack5 and allow users to try their luck at a significantly more rare and valuable card.
