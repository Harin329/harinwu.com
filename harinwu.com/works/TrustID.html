TrustID

Feb 2020



ROLE:

Full Stack Developer

TECHNOLOGY:

Swift, Herokyu, Postman, Bluetooth 4.0

COMPANY:

TELUS Internal Hackathon

DESCRIPTION:

Given the topic of Digital Identity, our team was able to leverage Bluetooth 4.0 technology in two phones to create an access door system. Using HTTPS standard encryption, biometrics, two-factor verification setup and issuer/holder/verifier flow, we were able to provide a demo showcasing its security. The user interfaces also doubles as a visual ID card. This was wrapped with a informative presentation to various senior TELUS architects.
