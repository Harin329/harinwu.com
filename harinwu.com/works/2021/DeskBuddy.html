DESCRIPTION:

A full system project starting from the basics like project planning, business plans, testing plans and internal + external design documents. The project was completed with a team of 8, receiving TA support for staying on track, as well as stakeholder meetings. The final product consists of three modules, a reservation platform with admin support for employees to book desks, a social platform with admin support to maintain engagement in a remote environment, and a mail management platform with admin support to handle employee mails and accept mail requests.
