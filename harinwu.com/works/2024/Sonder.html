Sonder

Sep 2023 - Jan 2025





Product:

Event Planning Platform

TECHNOLOGY:

ReactJS, React Native

COMPANY:

Personal Project

Sonder makes it easy to quickly propose, arrange, and send invites to friends and strangers. The app provides profiles that show the true you and help you meet your new bff. Sonder is built on the belief that real-life interactions are at the heart of cultivating genuine connection. Our platform is designed to facilitate gatherings to find new friends, help people live more fulfilling lives and build deeper, more intentional relationships.
