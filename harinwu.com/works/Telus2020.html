DESCRIPTION:

Working under Technology Strategy and Business Transformation, I am a co-op student on the Reporting Solutions team. I assisted with a year long plan to migrate a monolithic enterprise reporting application into a microservice architecture on the cloud. Evaluating privacy concerns with government contracts, I was able to help initiate a plan to gradually and safely migrate the application. This could ultimately provide the team with faster development and no downtime deployments, saving large amounts of money from service level agreement penalties.
